Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

We understand that choosing home care is an important decision. Here are answers to the questions families ask us most often.

What is the difference between Home Health Care and In-Home Care?

Home Health Care is medical care ordered by a physician and governed by specific eligibility guidelines. It is typically covered by Medicare or health insurance and is often short-term — usually following a hospital stay.

In-Home Care (also known as private duty care) is non-medical support for individuals who need assistance at home. While generally paid privately, some long-term care insurance policies may provide coverage. Services include companionship, assistance with daily activities, light housekeeping, and supervision.

Do I have to sign a long-term contract?

No. We offer flexible service options including 3-month, 6-month, and annual agreements, as well as as-needed services. You may discontinue services at any time without long-term obligation.

Clients are simply asked to agree not to hire our caregivers privately, to pay for services as agreed, and to provide a deposit to begin services — which is applied to your final invoice.

What are the benefits of choosing a locally owned agency?

As a locally owned and operated agency, Rocker Inspired Home Care provides personalized service with a strong focus on community, responsiveness, and building meaningful relationships — without the delays or complications often found with larger national organizations. We know our clients by name, and we treat every family like our own.

What types of clients do you serve?

We primarily serve seniors, but we also provide care for individuals of all ages. Our caregivers have experience supporting clients with a wide range of conditions, including:

  • Alzheimer's disease and dementia
  • Parkinson's disease
  • Stroke recovery
  • Diabetes
  • Arthritis
  • Heart conditions
  • Mobility challenges and more
How do I pay for services?

All payments are made directly to Rocker Inspired Home Care — not to individual caregivers. This ensures you are not responsible for employment taxes or insurance, as all caregivers are fully employed and insured through our agency. For your convenience, billing statements include a return envelope for easy payment.

How quickly can care services begin?

Once your care plan is finalized and a caregiver is assigned, services can typically begin within 24 hours. We understand that care needs can arise quickly, and we work hard to get your loved one the support they need without unnecessary delays.

Are your caregivers background-checked?

Absolutely. All Rocker Inspired Home Care caregivers undergo thorough background checks and drug screenings before being placed with any client. Your safety and peace of mind are our top priorities, and we take the screening process seriously.

What is the minimum number of hours per visit?

Our services require a minimum of 6 hours per day per visit. This ensures that caregivers have adequate time to provide thorough, quality care and to build a meaningful, consistent relationship with your loved one.
